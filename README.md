Most AI apps act as tracking tools in disguise. **R.ai is built on a strict privacy-first foundation.**

## 🚀 Core Features
### 🔒 100% Privacy & Local Storage
Your data is yours. R.ai stores all conversations, personas, and settings locally on your device. No cloud backups, no data harvesting.
### 📱 Autonomous App Control (WhatsApp & More)
R.ai acts as a true operator. By utilizing advanced Android Accessibility features, R.ai can autonomously navigate your phone, open apps like WhatsApp, and send messages on your behalf just like a real human finger.
### 🎭 Unlimited Custom Personas
Why talk to a generic robot? Create fully customized AI personas with unique system prompts. Whether you need a strict coding mentor, a creative writing coach, or a casual friend, R.ai adapts to you.
### 🔌 Multi-Provider AI Support
Don't be locked into one model. R.ai allows you to plug in your own API keys for various top-tier AI providers, giving you the freedom to choose the brain behind your assistant.
### 🔔 Smart Notification Reading
Stay hands-free. R.ai can securely hook into your device to read incoming notifications aloud, keeping you updated without ever needing to look at your screen.
### 🎙️ Advanced Voice Commands
Talk to your AI naturally. Enjoy fluid, conversational voice interactions with highly accurate speech-to-text processing.
### 🏠 Local Smart Home Hub (Home Assistant)
Control your physical environment. R.ai integrates directly with your local **Home Assistant** server. Turn off the lights, lock the doors, or check the temperature through natural language commands—all routed through your local network.
### 💎 Premium Glassmorphism UI
An AI should look as smart as it acts. R.ai features a breathtaking Glassmorphism aesthetic, featuring dynamic blurred backgrounds, smooth Lottie animations, and a responsive, buttery-smooth Flutter rendering engine.
